Why Zyno Overview

Founded in 2006, with headquarters based in the technology rich Boston, MA area Zyno Medical has sold, installed and supported thousands of its infusion pumps within alternate site infusion settings throughout the US.

We support our customers by way of a direct and specialized regional field sales & service team.

To ensure prompt delivery of product we operate from four regional distribution centers located across the U.S.

Built-in Expertise

This broad scope, long-term inside look at the infusion industry has provided Zyno Medical with unique expertise. We have leveraged this expertise into the core design and manufacturing of our Z-800 Infusion Pump Series, features and accessories.
We are also now introducing the availability of patient-centric, dispensing-to-documentation, wireless solutions for alternate site customers.
Our ZynoFlo Integrated Infusion System offers the ability to link Med Dispense Systems and EMR systems directly to Zyno infusion pumps providing entirely new levels of safety, workflow and business efficiencies.

Quality Counts

As you should expect, all Zyno Medical products adhere to strict FDA Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) and other regulatory guidelines to ensure product quality, performance and safety.
Additionally, Zyno Medical has made the extra investments and implemented process disciplines needed to meet additional ISO quality standards:

Zyno Medical is ISO 13485:2003 certified for the design, manufacture, distribution, installation and service of infusion pump systems, sterile IV sets and accessories.
